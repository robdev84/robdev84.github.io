Responding to Emergencies

high-visibility signs improve response times and save lives

Do you live at an unincorporated address? The West Chicago Fire Department has a high-visibility address sign to install on your mailbox to help emergency crews find your address easily in case of an emergency. The Fire Department will install the signs throughout the Spring of 2018. Prior to installing, the fire department will knock on your door to receive permission, however, if you use the form to give your authorization, your sign will be installed. You can give your permission here: Signs.

Keeping Our Community Safe

installations highlight close community partnerships

In partnership with the West Chicago Park District, the Foundation funded the purchase of Thor Guard early warning lightning detection systems for the baseball fields at Reed Keppler Park and Turtle Splash Aquatic Center. These systems warn of impending lightning strikes, giving patrons time to get to safety.
